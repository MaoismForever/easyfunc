javax.management.relation public class RelationNotification extends Notification A notification of a change in the Relation Service. A RelationNotification notification is sent when a relation is created via the Relation Service, or an MBean is added as a relation in the Relation Service, or a role is updated in a relation, or a relation is removed from the Relation Service.
